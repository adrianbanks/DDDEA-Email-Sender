Helpers on the day

As with all DDD events, DDD East Anglia is run entirely by volunteers. We are in need of room monitors to help out on the day. This involves supervising one of the rooms during a few sessions, and prompting the speaker as they approach the end of their alloted time. In return, we offer guaranteed attendance by pre-registering you for the conference before registration opens. If you would be interested in being a helper, please let us know be replying to this email.

Lunchtime "grok" talks

A tradition at many DDD events is for attendees to give "grok" talks during the lunch break. These are 5-10 minutes talks, and can be about any subject. If you would like to give a shortened version of your session in the form of a grok talk, or a talk about any other subject, please let us know.
